Laica's Positioning

Laica differentiates itself from 19 competitors (OMR Reviews, Appvizer, trusted.de, IT-Matchmaker, etc.) through a unique model combining neutral software advisory + procurement negotiation + KI-powered matching. While competitors offer self-service portals, Laica provides personalized consulting for KMUfocused on solving the 10 key pain points: DSGVO compliance, tool overload, lack of strategic guidance, and total cost of ownership.
